Our Process

Made Fresh, Daily.

You can't fake freshness. That's why our kitchen starts buzzing early every morning. We chop, mix, and season our fillings using locally-sourced ingredients. Then, our artisans carefully fold each momo, one by one, sealing in the flavour before they're steamed to perfection for you.

Our Journey

2023 - The Dream Begins

In a small kitchen in Gangtok, the first MoMo KulTure momo was born from a generations-old family recipe.

2024 - Gangtok & Soreng

We opened our first two outlets in Sikkim, quickly becoming a local favourite for authentic taste and quality.

2025 - Hello, Siliguri!

Our biggest step yet! We bring the authentic taste of the Himalayas to Siliguri with three brand new locations.

Future - The World!

Our mission is to share the true culture of momos with the world, one delicious, handcrafted parcel at a time.
